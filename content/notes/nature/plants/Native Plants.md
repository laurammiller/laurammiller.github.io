## Criteria
- Native to the northeastern US
- Flowering or nice color
- low sun/part shade tolerant
- clay soil tolerant
- perennial or self-seeding

## Groundcover
### creeping phlox
Flowers in early spring. Spreads quickly. Prefers sun. Grows to max of 6" high.

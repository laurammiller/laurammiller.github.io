# summary

An electronic device that converts energy from one form to another. For example, heat into voltage. Sound into frequency.

Examples:
- microphone
- speaker
- thermometer
- pressure sensor
- antenna
- [[notes/electrical/sensors/Thermocouple|Thermocouple]]
